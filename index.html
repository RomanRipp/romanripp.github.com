My Work

Here you will find information on my latest projects. I have also put some information on my past work.

We have studied the problem of coordinating an Unmanned Aerial Vehicle (UAV) and an Unmanned Ground Vehicle (UGV) for a precision agriculture application. Our goal is to estimate the N map over a field and classify each point based on N deficiency levels.

This appliaction for Google Glass I have developed for fun. It displays your acceleration in G, while allowing you to stay focused on your experience. Use it on roller coster, race track, or any extreme activity you are into. Remember, safety first!

My car speedometer got broken, as a result overnight it was replaced with an application for Google Glass. This app measures your speed in km/h or mph, shows your maximum speed and warns when you are about to break a speed limit.

This application is a recommender system for medical doctors. It takes in parameters of patients as an input and by means of machine learning algorithms produces likelihood estimation for disease complications.

I have studied applications of Google Glass for providing guidelines on defined procedures. Consider mechanic working on new type of engine or dental student learning new procedure. In this study I have evaluated experimental UI by means of controlled experiment with human subjects performing Origami task. I had to do some hacking to make interface comletely touch free. I have studied benefits of using voice activated UI coupled with HUD like display.

This is my current project on my favorite topic: robotics. I am creating a ROS based system for easy and reliable control of a UAV. My current setup is as follows: bebop parrot drone as UAV, raspberry PI 2 as a hardware controller, Oculus Rift as a FPV system, PS3 game controller as an input device, Blurex long range antena as communication device. I spare no time on this project and make emphaisis on testing and error handling. I am using unit testing extensively in order to cover as many emergency situations as possible before drone will get in the air. It is no secret that drones are becoming more and more popular. However issues in design make them dangerous and fragile in hands of unskilled pilots. I am attempting to solve this problem with my controller.
